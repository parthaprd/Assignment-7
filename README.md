# KeenKeeper — Keep Your Friendships Alive

KeenKeeper is a modern social health dashboard designed to help you nurture and maintain your most important friendships. In a world of digital noise, KeenKeeper helps you focus on meaningful connections through timely check-ins and interaction tracking.

## 🚀 Features

- **Friendship Dashboard:** A bird's-eye view of your social circle, highlighting who you haven't spoken to in a while.
- **Smart Status Tracking:** Friends are automatically categorized as *On-Track*, *Almost Due*, or *Overdue* based on your custom goals.
- **Quick Check-Ins:** Log calls, texts, or video chats with a single click to keep your history up to date.
- **Interaction Timeline:** A comprehensive history of your social interactions with powerful filtering and search.
- **Friendship Analytics:** Visual insights into your social habits using interactive charts.
- **Responsive Design:** Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop experiences.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **React 19:** For a fast, component-based user interface.
- **React Router 7:** For seamless navigation and deep linking.
- **Tailwind CSS:** For a modern, utility-first design system.
- **Recharts:** For beautiful, responsive data visualizations.
- **Lucide React:** For a consistent and expressive icon set.

- **Sonner:** For elegant toast notifications.

## 📈 Key Highlights

1. **Intentional Connection:** Move beyond passive scrolling to active relationship management.
2. **Data-Driven Insights:** Understand your communication patterns and improve your social health.
3. **Beautiful UI:** A clean, "glassmorphic" design that makes managing friendships a delight.
